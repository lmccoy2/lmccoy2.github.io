The Town I Grew Up In, Camp Hill

We moved from Georgia to Pennsylvania a couple months before I was one year old. We came to Camp Hill when I was around 3 years old. Since then, I have lived in this same town for most of my life. We have stayed in the same home for around a decade. What I like about this town the most is how its very peaceful and friendly and still has many big businesses within it.
